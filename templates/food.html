Top 10 Most Popular Somali Foods & Drinks

Canjeero / Anjero

A staple sourdough pancake, often served with honey, ghee, or stews.

Sambusa

A triangular fried or baked pastry filled with spiced meat or vegetables.

Bariis (Somali Rice)

Fragrant spiced rice cooked with cumin, cardamom, and sometimes raisins.

Shaah (Somali Tea)

Sweet, spiced tea infused with cardamom, cloves, and other warming spices.

Camel Milk

A key part of Somali pastoral life, often enjoyed fresh or slightly fermented.

Xalwo (Halwa)

A sweet confection made from sugar, cornstarch, ghee, and spices like cardamom.

Baasto

A Somali pasta dish, often served with a rich tomato sauce and aromatic spices.

Oodkac

A traditional dish featuring tender meat slow-cooked with herbs and spices.

Sabaayad & Maraq

Sabaayad, a savory flatbread, paired with a hearty stew (maraq) bursting with flavor.

Soor & Hilib Ari

A traditional dish combining a porridge-like soor with succulent goat meat (hilib ari) and spices.
